🎍 Certification

Pass mark is 80 out of 100 (Knowledge Quiz 50 pts + Practical Assessment 50 pts). Certificates will be issued via Google Classroom and WhatsApp within 48 hours of the session. Participants who score below 80% will be contacted to arrange a 30-minute remediation session before re-attempting the practical assessment only.
