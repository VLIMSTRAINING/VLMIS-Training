Session 1 · Job Aid

Adjustments

Record losses, expiries, damages and stock corrections

ℹ

System applies credit or debit automatically

You do not choose whether stock increases or decreases. The system does this based on the reason you select. Always pick the most specific reason available.

Adjustment reasons

Reduces

Expired

Vials past their expiry date

Reduces

Damaged

Broken or physically compromised vials

Reduces

VVM Failed

Vaccine Vial Monitor shows heat exposure

Reduces

Missing

On system but cannot be located physically

Increases

Found in Store

Physical stock found not on system records

Depends

Other Adjustment

Any other correction — always add a description

Steps

1

Open Adjustments

Tap Adjustments → select programme → enter date → tap Proceed.

2

Select product & batch

Find the product in the list and tap it. Select the correct batch.

3

Enter reason & quantity

  • Reason: select from dropdown — most specific reason available
  • Quantity: number of doses affected
  • System automatically applies the credit or debit
4

Submit & confirm

Tap Submit → confirm. The bin card records the change with date and reason for full traceability.

✓ Practice checklist

  • Open Adjustments → select product → select TRAINING001 batch
  • Record: reason = Expired, quantity = 3 → verify SOH reduced
  • Record: reason = Found in Store, quantity = 2 → verify SOH increased
  • Open the bin card and confirm both adjustments appear
