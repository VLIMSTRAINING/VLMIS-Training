Sign In & First Login

Authentication · Offline access · Facility check

⚠

First login needs internet

Your very first sign-in downloads all reference data. You must be connected to Wi-Fi or mobile data. Every login after that works offline.

Steps to sign in

1

Open the VLMIS app

Tap the app icon. The Sign In screen loads automatically.

2

Enter your credentials

Type your registered email address and password (provided by your trainer). Tap Sign In.

3

Wait for reference data download

First login only: the app downloads all reference data. Takes 2–5 minutes. Do not close the app.

4

Confirm your facility

Your name and facility appear at the top of the home screen. Confirm both are correct before entering any data. If wrong — contact your administrator.

✓

Forgot password?

Tap Forgot Password on the Sign In screen → enter your email → follow the reset link in your inbox.

✓ Practice checklist

  • Open the app and sign in with your UAT credentials
  • Confirm your name and facility appear correctly
  • Navigate back to the home screen — identify all 9 quick links
