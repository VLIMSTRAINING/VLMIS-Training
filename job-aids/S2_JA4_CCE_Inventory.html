Steps: viewing & updating CCE status

1

Open CCE Inventory

Tap ☰ (drawer menu) → tap CCE Inventory. All equipment registered at your facility appears with current status.

2

View equipment details

Tap any item to see its full record: type, model, serial number, installation date, and current operational status.

3

Tap Update Status

  • Tap the Update Status button on the item detail page
  • A status form slides up from the bottom of the screen
  • Select: Working / Not Working / Needs Service
  • Add a comment describing the issue or repair
  • Tap Save
4

Verify

The list updates immediately. Change saves locally and syncs automatically when online — no manual upload needed.

ℹ

Equipment not in the list?

Contact your provincial or district administrator to register it. Give them: equipment type, model, and serial number from the manufacturer plate.

✓ Practice checklist

  • Open drawer menu (☰) → CCE Inventory
  • View full details of one equipment item
  • Tap Update Status → change to Not Working → add a comment
  • Save → verify status updated in the list
  • Tap Update Status again → change back to Working
